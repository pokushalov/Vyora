Vyora Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

Data Collection

Vyora does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information. All image and video files are processed entirely on your device.

Network Access

Vyora does not access the internet. It operates fully offline with no network connections.

File Access

Vyora accesses only the files and folders you explicitly open through the system file picker or drag-and-drop. File paths of recently opened items are stored locally on your device using Security-Scoped Bookmarks and are never transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

Vyora does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
